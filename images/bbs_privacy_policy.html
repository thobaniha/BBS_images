Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Beauty Booking System (BBS) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the BBS app, we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information (Users)

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data (used for booking and service availability)
  • Other profile-related data you provide voluntarily

Salon and Service Provider Information

  • Salon service details including:
    • Service name
    • Price
    • Duration of the service
    • Description and key features
  • Employee details who perform services, including:
    • Employee name
    • Cellphone number

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Facilitate salon bookings and manage services
  • Enable location-based service suggestions
  • Improve app performance and customer support

3. Ratings and Reviews

Users may provide ratings for services after their appointments. These ratings are collected anonymously and used to improve service quality. No personal information is attached to the ratings unless voluntarily disclosed in a comment or review.

4. Firebase

Our app uses Firebase, a backend service provided by Google. Firebase securely stores your data and helps manage authentication, real-time data, and cloud services. You can read more about Firebase's privacy practices here.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information or service data with any third parties. All collected data is used exclusively within the BBS system to enable bookings, manage services, and improve user experience.

6. Children's Privacy

While our app is not specifically designed for children under 13, it does not contain any harmful content. Parents or guardians concerned about data collected from minors may contact us to address any concerns.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: beautybookingsystem@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. You are encouraged to review this policy regularly.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: beautybookingsystem@gmail.com
